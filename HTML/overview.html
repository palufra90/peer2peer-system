Progetto SD -Distributed Computing System-

Erlang nodes connect to Riak cluster by knowing at least one Riak node. Erlang Nodes can insert jobs in DHT to make them being executed by other nodes in the cluster. Erlang Nodes can both insert and execute jobs.

Module dht

DHT management: read, write, update and delete operations.

Module connect

Connection management and message handling.

Module trade

Job management: request, assignment and execution.

Module aux

Auxiliar functions.

dht.hrl

Record and macro definitions.
